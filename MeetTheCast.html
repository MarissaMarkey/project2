Meet the Cast

Something very surprising about the musical Dear Evan Hansen is that there are only eight actors/actresses in the entire cast. Of course, there are understudies and people that work behind the scenes but there are technically only eight people. Unlike most of the other Broadway musicals, this musical is able to create the entire story with only eight people, and these eight people pull off the characters amazingly. Dear Evan Hansen is on a national tour right now, so each character has a different actor for this tour. Although it is not the original cast, they still do a wonderful job of sharing Evan's story.


Benn Platt: Are there any Pitch Perfect fans out there? Well, if you are, you might recognize Benn Platt from those movies. Platt plays the main character (Evan) in Dear Evan Hansen. He is 25 years old, and was born in Los Angeles California. He has been acting for most of his life in movie such as Pitch Perfect and the Book of Morman. Platt won a Tony for Best Actor in a Musical, for his performance in Dear Evan Hansen.


Laura Dreyfuss: Dreyfuss plays Zoe Murphy(Evan's love interest) in the play Dear Evan Hansen. She got her BFA in musical theater from the Boston Conservatory. She is now 30 years old, and has been acting professionally since 2010.


Kristolyn Lloyd: This is a 33 year old actress who was born and raised just outside of Houston Texas. She graduated from the Carnegie Mellon University where she received her Bachelor's Degree in Drama. She is mostly known for her role as Alana Beck(Evan's friend) in Dear Evan Hansen.


Will Roland: Roland plays Jared Kleinman, Evan's family friend, in Dear Evan Hansen. He is 29 years old and has a singing degree from the Steinhardt School of Culture, Education, and Human Development. He started his acting career in 2015, in an episode of The Mysteries of Laura.


Rachel Bay Jones: Jones is an American actress and singer. She is 48 years old and has been in many different Broadway musicals during her career. In Dear Evan Hansen, she plays Heidi Hanse (Evan's caring mom). In 2016, she won the Tony for Best Featured Actress in a Musical.


Mike Feist: Feist is only 26 years old making him the youngest actor in the original Dear Evan Hansen cast, but he is already playing one of the lead characters on Dear Evan Hansen. He plays Conner Murphy (a boy from Evan's school who Evan is not very close with). Fiest was adopted at birth, and gre up in Gahanna, Ohio. He went to the American Musical and Dramatic Academy but dropped out after two years to start auditioning for shows. He was nominated for a Tony for Best Featured actor in a musical.


Micheal Park: Park is an American actor who is 50 years old. He attended Nazareth College, originally wanting to become a architect before starting acting. In Dear Evan Hansen, he plays Larry Murphy (Conner's dad). He has been in many in diferent TV shows, and now lives in New York with his wife and three children.


Jennifer Laura Thompson: This actress is 48 years old, and she made her Broadway debut in the original cast of Footloose. She has been on many Broadway shows including playing Glinda in Wicked. In Dear Evan Hansen, she plays Cynthia Murphy (Conner's mom).
